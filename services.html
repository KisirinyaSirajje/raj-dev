What We Offer

At Raj Dev, we provide a wide range of digital services designed to help your business thrive in the online world. From custom web development to mobile applications and digital marketing, our team of experts is ready to bring your vision to life.

Each service is tailored to meet your specific needs and goals, ensuring that you receive a solution that drives real results for your business.

Web Development

We create custom, responsive websites that look great on all devices and are optimized for performance and user experience. Our web development services include:

  • Custom website design and development
  • E-commerce websites
  • Content Management Systems (WordPress, Joomla, etc.)
  • Web application development
  • Website maintenance and support

Using the latest technologies like HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, and more, we build websites that not only look great but also perform exceptionally well.

Mobile App Development

Extend your reach with custom mobile applications that engage your customers and streamline your business processes. Our mobile development services include:

We focus on creating intuitive, high-performance apps that provide real value to users and help your business grow.

UI/UX Design

Great design is at the heart of successful digital products. Our design team creates beautiful, user-centered interfaces that enhance user experience and drive engagement. Our UI/UX services include:

We believe in design that not only looks good but also serves a purpose, making your digital products both beautiful and functional.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services

How long does it take to complete a website?

The timeline for website development varies depending on the complexity and scope of the project. A basic website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while more complex projects with custom functionality may take 8-12 weeks or more. During our initial consultation, we'll provide you with a more accurate timeline based on your specific requirements.

Do you provide website maintenance services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive website maintenance services to ensure your site remains secure, up-to-date, and performing optimally. Our maintenance packages include regular updates, security monitoring, backups, performance optimization, and technical support.

Can you help with an existing website?

Absolutely! We can help improve, update, or redesign your existing website. Our team will assess your current site and recommend the best approach to enhance its design, functionality, performance, and user experience.

What is your payment structure?

We typically require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining balance due upon project completion. For larger projects, we may establish a milestone-based payment schedule. We accept bank transfers and mobile money payments.

Do you provide hosting services?

Yes, we offer reliable web hosting services with excellent uptime, security features, and technical support. Our hosting packages are designed to ensure your website loads quickly and remains secure. We can also work with your existing hosting provider if preferred.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! All websites we develop are fully responsive and mobile-friendly, ensuring they look and function perfectly on all devices, including smartphones, tablets, laptops, and desktop computers. Mobile optimization is a standard part of our development process.
